Cost Savings

It seems a bit of a paradox: add an additional link to your graphic production chain and still save money? And yet it is true. By handing over the cost of creating production files to PPC instead of your creative graphics agency you will already save quite a bit. Not only are our hourly costs lower than that of a design agency, but our people are also trained to work quickly and efficiently. They complete more production work in the same hour. And so you save double.

Last but not least, PPC delivers a 100% reliable, predictable end product. In this way you prevent a situation where the printed matter does not meet your requirements and has to be printed again. A cost that you obviously want to avoid.
