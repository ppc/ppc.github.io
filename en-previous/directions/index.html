Directions

Coming from Antwerp

  1. Follow the E17 motorway in the direction of Ghent.
  2. Take exit 15 to St-Niklaas. You will automatically join the N16.
  3. Keep to the extreme right-hand lane and exit by following the arrows marked Oostjachtpark, making a 180-degree turn.
  4. Take a left-turn just before the Jacobs Mercedes garage.
  5. Follow this road to a T-Junction. Turn right. Drive to the next T-junction again and turn right.
  6. After 50 metres, the first street on your left leads to the Oostjachtpark.
  7. Drive straight on, past the first street on your right. From this point you can already see our buildings on your left.
